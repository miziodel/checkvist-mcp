# User Persona: The Productivity Architect

## Core Identity
The **Productivity Architect** is a power user who treats their task manager not just as a to-do list, but as a custom-built operating system for their life and work. They are often developers, researchers, or managers of complex projects who value speed, hierarchy, and keyboard-driven efficiency.
